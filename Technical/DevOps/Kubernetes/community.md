# Community
- **K8s enhancement proposal (KEP):** a proposal procedure of k8s community.
- **Kubernetes Special Interest Group (SIG):** are volunteers from the k8s community who have expertise and interest in a particular area of the platform. They are responsible for developing and maintaining specific components or features within k8s.
- **Steering committee:** make decision as key issues.
- **Working group:** team come together to work on a specific issue.
